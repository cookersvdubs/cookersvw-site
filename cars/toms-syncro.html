Tom's syncro

Remember Tom? We recently finished a patina restoration on his '64 bus? Well here is his 4X4. This thing is wild. Check this out: Subaru 3.0l H6 engine, original VW transaxle with modified gearing and a Weddle transaxle cooling system. lockers in front and rear with a decoupler. Porsche 930 axles. Audi A8 brakes with a BMW booster. Ford F250 rear springs on Abercrombie articulated perches. Burley
