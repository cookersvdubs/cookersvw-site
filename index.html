Who We Are

A Family Shop.
A Real Passion.

We're air-cooled VW specialists based in Williamsport, Maryland — restoring Bugs, Buses, Ghias, and everything else Volkswagen ever built. We do it because we love it, and it shows in every car we touch.

Our story caught the attention of National Public Radio — not because we set out to be famous, but because what we do here is genuine. Whether it's a stock engine rebuild or a full off-pan restoration, every car gets our best.
