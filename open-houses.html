Open Houses

Gather at the Shop

Three Events. One Community.

Cooker's has hosted open house events that bring together the air-cooled VW community in Western Maryland. Cars fill the lot, friends reconnect, and everyone gets a look at what's happening inside the shop. Below are the photo galleries from each event.
